About Spiral Drawings

Spiral drawing tests are widely used in clinical practice to assess motor impairments associated with Parkinson's Disease. Patients with PD often produce distorted spirals due to:

  • Tremors
  • Bradykinesia (slowness of movement)
  • Reduced drawing smoothness
  • Micrographia (small handwriting)
