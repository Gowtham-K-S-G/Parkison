How It Works

Our AI-powered system uses Convolutional Neural Networks to analyze spiral drawings

1

Upload Image

Upload a clear image of a spiral drawing. The image can be captured using a smartphone camera or scanned from paper.

  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, BMP
  • Clear, well-lit images work best
  • Include the entire spiral in frame
2

AI Analysis

Our CNN model processes the image using advanced computer vision techniques to extract meaningful features.

  • Image preprocessing
  • Feature extraction
  • Pattern recognition
  • Deep learning classification
3

Get Results

Receive instant prediction with confidence score and probability distribution for both classes.

  • Instant predictions
  • Confidence score
  • Probability distribution
  • Recommendations

Technical Details

CNN Architecture
  • Input: 128x128 grayscale images
  • 4 Convolutional blocks with BatchNormalization
  • MaxPooling and Dropout for regularization
  • Fully connected layers with softmax output
Preprocessing Pipeline
  • Image resizing to 128x128
  • Grayscale conversion
  • Gaussian blur for noise reduction
  • Adaptive thresholding
  • Pixel normalization

Spiral Drawing Tips

For Best Results:
  • Use white paper for contrast
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Keep the spiral centered
  • Use a dark pen or pencil
  • Avoid shadows on the image
What We Analyze:
  • Irregular patterns in the spiral
  • Tremor-induced distortions
  • Drawing smoothness
  • Line quality variations
  • Overall shape consistency
